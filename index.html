Zakat is one of the five pillars of Islam, meant to purify wealth and support the less fortunate.

Understanding Zakat

What is Zakat?

Zakat is a form of almsgiving to purify wealth and help those in need.

Types of Zakat

Different forms of zakat include zakat on wealth, gold, business, and more.

Importance of Zakat

Zakat helps in wealth distribution, promoting social justice and reducing poverty.
